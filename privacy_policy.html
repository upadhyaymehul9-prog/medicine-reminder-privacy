Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Medicine Reminder ("the App") is developed by Mehul Upadhyay. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

2. No Data Collection

Medicine Reminder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server or third party. The App works completely offline.

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Medicine names and reminder times you enter
  • Your preferred language for voice reminders
  • Dose log history (kept for 30 days, then automatically deleted)
  • App settings (voice on/off, popup on/off)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Permissions Used

  • Notifications — To show medicine reminders
  • Alarms & Reminders — To fire reminders at exact scheduled times
  • Boot Completed — To reschedule reminders after phone restart
  • Vibration — To vibrate with reminder notifications
  • Foreground Service — To speak voice reminders using Text-to-Speech
  • Battery Optimization — To ensure reminders work reliably in background

None of these permissions are used to collect or share your data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No internet connection is required or used.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
